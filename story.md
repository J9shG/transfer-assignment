# The Digital Lifeboat

I stared at the screen, my vision blurring as the 'File Not Found' error mocked me. Weeks of coding, thousands of lines of logic, and countless late nights felt like they had vanished into a digital void. My stomach did a slow, painful somersault as I realized my local drive had finally given up on me.

Just as I was about to spiral into total despair, I remembered my ritual. Every evening before closing my laptop, I had typed those three magic words into my terminal. I quickly logged into my GitHub account from my tablet, and there it was—my entire repository, safely tucked away in the cloud thanks to my last **git push**. The relief washed over me like a cold wave on a summer day.
